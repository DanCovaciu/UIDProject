HOW?

These are some of the most important things that you need to do when starting this journey of energy efficiency. Complete these steps and you're almost there!

  • Buy the number of solar panels needed for your house.
  • Purchase a battery bank.
  • Have DC-to-AC inverters in your solar system.
  • Have a battery monitor.
  • Include disconnect switches in your system.
